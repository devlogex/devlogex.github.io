With the evolution of AI, our approach and mindset toward work need to change significantly. We should shift from asking "What to do" to "How to do it."

## Why is this change important?

Most people are used to the "What to do" mindset. When we receive a ticket, request, or question, our first thought is usually: "What should I do to complete this?" This approach works most of the time. However, the problem is that it doesn't improve our workflow. Even after years of working, our productivity may remain the same.

The "What to do" mindset is simple and easy, which is why it becomes a habit. But this habit can turn into a barrier—a major blocker that prevents us from reaching the next level of productivity.

In contrast, the "How to do it" mindset is more challenging. It requires us to think more deeply: "How can this type of task be done more effectively?" This kind of thinking encourages us to design better workflows and become more productive—especially with the support of AI tools.

Humans cannot execute tasks as quickly as AI. But with a well-designed workflow, we can significantly increase our productivity by leveraging AI effectively.

Developing the habit of thinking "How to do it" first is essential to staying relevant in this rapidly changing world. If we don't adapt, we risk falling behind.

Admittedly, changing this mindset is not easy. It may even take more time at first, especially for tasks we are already familiar with. However, the long-term benefits are worth it.

So starting now, let's train ourselves to think "How to do it" whenever we receive any task, request, or question—even the simplest ones. It may take more time in the beginning, but it will save us a lot more time in the future.
